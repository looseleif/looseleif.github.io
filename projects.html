Featured Projects

AGGRO-9000

Sep 2022 - Jul 2024

Associated with University of Minnesota

AGGRO is an advanced robotic system designed to handle highly occluded mechanical search tasks. Utilizing deep learning models, AGGRO enhances search efficiency in cluttered environments.

Open Sauce 2024 Showcase

June 2024

Open Sauce 2024 was an exciting platform where I showcased several of my key projects, including the AGGRO Robotic System, the aWear Cardiac Monitor, and The Feedback Loop homeostasis device. It was a great opportunity to connect with like-minded individuals, share innovations, and gain feedback from the broader community.

aWear - Wearable Cardiac Monitor

Jan 2023 - May 2023

Associated with University of Minnesota

aWear is a cardiac monitoring system focused on detecting arrhythmias in real-time, enabling medical interventions in critical situations. The system features a populated PCB, PPG sensors, and an interactive poster to demonstrate its functionality.

The Feedback Loop - Educational and Interactive Homeostasis Device

Mar 2022 - Mar 2023

Associated with University of Minnesota

This project was designed as an educational tool for understanding human homeostasis. It included initial bring-up, prototyping, and the development of the first batch of devices, aimed at making learning about biology interactive and fun.

DIY All-Terrain Electric Longboard

Jun 2021 - Jun 2022

This project involved designing and building a DIY all-terrain electric longboard from scratch, including components such as the battery, motors, and control systems. Bench testing and the maiden voyage were key milestones in the journey toward building a highly functional electric longboard.
